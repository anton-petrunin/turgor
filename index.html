The Tournament of Towns, State College, PA,

The Tournament of Towns, an international mathematical competition for school students.

  • Local organizers: Alexei Novikov and Anton Petrunin
  • Where:
    • Room 101 at Math Department McAllister Building, or on the Campus Map.
  • When: 11am–4pm (you can leave earlier)
    • O-level (basic): October 15, 2023
    • A-level (advanced): October 29, 2023
  • Before you come: At least two days in advance, please send an email to turgor.sc.2016@zoho.com that includes the following information:
    • Student's name.
    • Level (A, O or both).
    • Student's grade.

The A-level is comparable in difficulty to with the International Mathematical Olympiads; the O-level is simpler.
Only the best scores for three problems are taken into account.
