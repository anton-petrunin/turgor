The Tournament of Towns, State College, PA, 2019

The Tournament of Towns, an international mathematical competition for school students.

  • Local organizers: Alexei Novikov and Anton Petrunin
  • Where:
    • Room 101 at Math Department McAllister Building, or on the Campus Map.
  • When: 11am–4pm (you can leave earlier)
    • O-level (basic): October 13, 2019
    • A-level (advanced): October 27, 2019
  • Before you come: Please write an email to turgor.sc.2016@zoho.com which includes
    • Student's name.
    • Level (A, O or both).
    • Student's grade.

The A-level is comparable in its difficulty with the International Mathematical Olympiads, the O-level is somewhat simpler.
On each level of each round, only the best scores for 3 problems are taken into account.
